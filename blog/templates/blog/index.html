Fictional character biography


Powers and abilities

Iron Man 2020 wears a suit of highly sophisticated armor,
similar in appearance to the classic Iron Man armor
(circa Model 5), with the exception of the faceplate and
shoulders. Beam weapons are fitted in the gauntlets of
the suit, as well as the chest beam and shoulder epaulets.
The user no longer requires a cybernetic link in the helmet
to control the armor; the helmet can also be hidden via
cloaking technology, which was presumably developed based
on the Chameleon mode of the Silver Centurion armor. Iron
Man 2020's systems are built for warfare, not super heroics
like the original Iron Man. Iron Man 2020's repulsors
(and an arsenal of other weapons) always fire at maximum
power for the most lethal effects. Curiously, Iron Man 2020
also has the original Iron Man's 1970s-era rollerskates
(updated to a "roller blade" style (see ASM Annual #20))
built into the boots of the suit.
